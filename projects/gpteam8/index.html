Concept:

This car was designed for a competition in which cars are timed on a course with 1"-wide white tape. At UC San Diego, this competition is called GrandPrIEEE but at other schools the competition has different names, such as NATCAR at UC Davis. The competition rules state that the car must be electrically powered, have four or fewer wheels, and have a home-built motor controller using discrete components and ICs.
